Twitter privacy policy
Please read this information before scrolling down

It's hard to make everyone happy with the Privacy Policy. Most Twitter users want to read something short and easy to understand. And while we'd love to fit everything you need to know into one Tweet, our regulators require us to comply with a legal obligation to be detailed.

Knowing this, we have written our Privacy Policy in the most accessible form so that you can make informed decisions when using Twitter, making sure that you control the information we collect, understand how it is used and when such information is shared.
